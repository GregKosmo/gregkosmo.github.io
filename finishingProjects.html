Finishing My Projects

09/23/2023

After six years of my development career, I've started many side projects. Most have not evolved beyond a domain name. I've decided to challenge myself to complete these projects and document my experience along the way. My current work experience consists primarily of:
  • 4+ years of Angular experience
  • 4+ years of Node experience
  • 3+ years of Docker experience
  • 1 year of Vue experience
During this journey I plan on starting with what I know, but hope to expand my knowledge to new areas as well. Ultimately, my goal is to prove to myself I'm still capable of producing products that fit the criteria of:
  • Useful
  • Not overly-complex
  • Quick to market
As I think those are the marks of a good product. After all, an idea is only as good as how quickly you can execute it. The links below document my experience working through these projects.
